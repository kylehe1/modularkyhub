Phantasm Demontia – The Game

Enter a haunting, pixelated world in this 2D metroidvania-style game. Choose between two powerful Generals — Feruzula or Erngarr — and battle through sectors of the Neo Empire, overrun by the zombie force known as Condemnia.
The story follows events from the Kaiden's Nightmare Arc in the first book, reimagined through atmospheric exploration, fast-paced combat, and narrative-rich environments. Each level is handcrafted with Asperite.
