Phantasm Demontia – Incomplete

A 2D metroidvania-style game. Choose between two of the 7 Generals — Feruzula or Erngarr — and battle through multiple sectors of the Neo Empire, overrun by the undead force known as Condemnia.
The story follows events from Kaiden's Nightmare arc in the first book, reimagined through atmospheric exploration, fast-paced combat, and narrative-rich environments.
Each level was handcrafted with Asperite.
One day it will be complete and on Steam. One day. But I am currently focused on finishing my books first.
Perhaps if I had more collaborations, it would finally be complete. But for now. BOOK!
