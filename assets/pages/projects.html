Phantasm Demontia – The Game

The game starts with you picking your favorite General. Right now there are only two to choose from: Feruzula and Erngarr.
You go through sector by sector of the Neo Empire, fighting the Condemnia infection as told in Kaiden's Nightmare Arc of the first book — all in 2D pixel side-scrolling action!
